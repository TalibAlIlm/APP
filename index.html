Welcome, Talib al-ʿIlm

Your Path to Islamic Scholarship

❀ On Intention — al-Niyyah

Before opening any book, check your heart. You are not studying to debate, to gain status, or to be called a scholar. You are studying to act upon what you learn and to draw closer to Allah. The one who learns without acting is warned in the Quran itself (Surah al-Saff 61:2–3).

📖 Study with a Qualified Shaykh

This roadmap is a guide only. It does not replace a living teacher with a connected chain of transmission (isnād) and sound creed (ʿaqīdah). Seek a Shaykh who combines knowledge with practice, and whose methodology aligns with the Quran and authentic Sunnah as understood by the Salaf al-Salih. If no local teacher is available, seek the authenticated programs of IUM Madinah, IMSIU Riyadh, or Umm al-Qura Makkah.

Choose Your Specializations

This step is optional. The Qur'an and the core mutūn are memorized by every student; selecting a specialization (takhaṣṣuṣ) simply adds its advanced texts to your ❀ memorization plan. Pick as many as you wish — or none, to keep only the core ḥifẓ.

Choose Your Fiqh Specialization

All four madhabs follow the Salafi ʿaqīdah. Choose your fiqh track. All other sciences remain fully visible for everyone.

You can change your specialization at any time via the "Track" button in the header.
